## COLOR & TONE DIRECTION

**Palette mood:** Dark base (#0A0A0F or deep navy-black) + iridescent accent gradients (aurora-style: violet → cyan → emerald). Think oil-on-water. Not neon, not loud — *luminous*.

**Typography feel:** Narrow, elegant display font (e.g. Neue Haas Grotesk, Aktiv Grotesk, or similar condensed grotesque) + a lighter body weight. Lots of whitespace. No decorative clutter.

**Voice:** Confident. Sparse. Every line earns its place.

## WRITING GUIDELINES (for Designer/Developer Reference)

- **Tone:** Confident, minimal, no fluff. Write like the platform is already proven.
- **Headlines:** Short, punchy, often broken across 2 lines for rhythm. No full stops in headlines.
- **Body copy:** Maximum 3 sentences per block. Prefer shorter.
- **Avoid:** Buzzwords like "revolutionary," "seamless," "game-changer." Let features speak.
- **CTAs:** Action-oriented, direct. "Request a Demo" > "Learn More."
- **Numbers:** Use them when real. Don't fabricate.
